Short, direct answers. The longer ones we cover on the call.

Why would I run this with an outside operator instead of in-house?

Because the team you have is already running the business. AAA Accelerator is fulfilling and selling. The AIOS workshop and coaching layer is shipping. Morningside AI is delivering client work. Adding a 90-day ladder build, a premium-tier launch, mid-tier SKUs, and a paid acquisition layer on top of an already-loaded ops team is how those projects either get postponed by a year or get launched at half the quality. Outside operator is faster, cleaner, and exits when production hits steady state. We hand the system to your team after the launch cycle, not before.

Will the AIOS Operator Cohort cannibalise AAA Accelerator?

No. The Cohort and AAA solve different problems. AAA Accelerator is the productised 6-month curriculum for operators starting or scaling their first AI agency. The Cohort is strategic operating-room access for operators who already run agencies past $20K MRR and want to scale into AIOS deployments at portfolio scale. Different deliverable, different audience, different buying motion. Most Cohort applicants will be AAA graduates moving up the ladder, not net-new buyers choosing Cohort over AAA. Where there's overlap, the Cohort is the explicit "post-AAA" tier. It expands the ladder, it doesn't compete inside it.

How does the mid-tier SKU not cannibalise AAA Accelerator?

The mid-tier SKU is positioned and priced for the buyer who would never have booked an AAA call. Friction-averse, lower-ticket commitment, no coaching, no community access, just the curriculum or the modules. The AAA buyer who would have closed on the phone-sales call still closes there. The mid-tier captures the segment currently leaking out before they ever reach the call. We track buyer source and conversion path from week one so the cannibalisation question gets answered with data, not assumption. If the mid-tier ever shows AAA buyer drag, we adjust positioning or price the same week.

How does this work alongside Morningside AI?

Morningside is the credibility anchor and the agency Liam-the-operator runs. The build doesn't touch Morningside delivery. What it does is thread Morningside case studies, deployment stats, and operator footage into both the AAA and AIOS funnels as proof. Right now Morningside is the thing that makes you different from every other AAA teacher, and the funnels don't lean on it. After the build they do. The agency keeps running exactly as it does today.

What if the Cohort doesn't fill?
